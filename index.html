Rules

  • Use the arrow keys to slide tiles up, down, left, or right across the board.
  • When two tiles labeled with the same number collide, they will merge into one tile containing the product of those two numbers.
  • After the player slides the tiles across the game board, a new tile labeled with a 2 or a 4 will appear in a random empty space on the board.
  • To win the game, create a tile containing the number 2048.
